Privacy Policy

When you access the Website, We’ll learn certain information about you during your visit:

b. Information we collect through Google Analytics. This data will generally include information about your location, how you’re using our website, what device you’re using, etc. The information we collect automatically is used for statistical data to improve our Website and our service offerings and won’t include personal information.

This Website uses cookies and similar technologies (Google Analytics) to provide us with information on how people move about the site, what is of interest to them and what is not, how our marketing is performing, and incidental items such as what percentage of users access the site from a personal computer versus a mobile phone. You may block most cookies by adjusting your browser settings.

Some content or applications on the Website are served by third-parties. These third parties may use cookies alone or in conjunction with web beacons or other tracking technologies to collect information about you when you use my website. We do not control these third parties’ tracking technologies or how they may be used. If you have any questions about this, you should contact the responsible provider directly.

4. How long do We keep your data?

We will retain any information you choose to provide to me until the earlier of:

● You asking us to delete the information,
● Our decision to cease using our existing data providers, or
● We decide that the value in retaining the data is outweighed by the costs of retaining it.
